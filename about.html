Lions

Lions are known as the kings of the jungle. They live in groups called prides and are known for their strength and majestic appearance.

Dolphins

Dolphins are intelligent marine mammals known for their playful behavior. They communicate through a series of clicks and whistles.

Elephants

Elephants are the largest land mammals, known for their strong social bonds and impressive memory. They are also highly intelligent creatures.
